# Training Requirements
- Be a US Lacrosse member
- Attend training classes
- Pass US Lacrosse annually
- Attend field training dates for 2021 from [home](/) page

# Annual Renewal Requirements
- Be a US Lacrosse member
- Pass US Lacrosse annually
- Attend pre-season scrimages
